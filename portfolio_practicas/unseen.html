Unseen

Unseen is a deeply personal photography project inspired by my experiences with depression and anxiety. It explores the emotional journey of facing what is often invisible to others.


The series is mostly in black and white, reflecting the intensity of these emotions. Cold, foggy landscapes create a sense of isolation while the central figure moves through different emotional stages.
