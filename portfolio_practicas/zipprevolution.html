LUZ ZIPP REVOLUTION 4 SEASONS UNSEEN SEO BIRDLIFE SABON THE LITTLE PRINCE

This project showcases a video recording focused on the athletic and recreational use of the bicycle. The audiovisual piece combines dynamic camera angles, fluid motion shots, and seamless editing techniques to convey a strong sense of energy, momentum, and visual storytelling.


Throughout the production, great care was taken to capture the excitement, rhythm, and speed inherent in the cycling experience, while maintaining a visually aesthetic and cohesive style.
