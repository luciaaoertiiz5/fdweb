LUZ ZIPP REVOLUTION 4 SEASONS UNSEEN SEO BIRDLIFE SABON THE LITTLE PRINCE

This project involved the design of a brochure and a poster, with the poster placed on the reverse side of the brochure. The Sabon typeface was chosen for its elegant and classical character. The design explores a minimalist approach, using blue as the main color to offer a fresh and contemporary interpretation while respecting the essence of the typeface.
