Zipp Revolution

This project showcases a video recording focused on the athletic and recreational use of the bicycle. The audiovisual piece combines dynamic camera angles, fluid motion shots, and seamless editing techniques.


Great care was taken to capture the excitement and rhythm inherent in the cycling experience, while maintaining a visually aesthetic and cohesive style through visual storytelling.
