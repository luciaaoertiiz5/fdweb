SEO BirdLife

This project focuses on the rebranding of SEO/BirdLife, an NGO dedicated to the protection of nature and biodiversity.


The goal was to bring light and clarity to the brand through a contemporary visual system. Color, typography, and graphic elements were carefully chosen to reflect nature, transparency, and accessibility.
