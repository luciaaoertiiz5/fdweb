ZIPP REVOLUTION 4 SEASONS UNSEEN SEO BIRDLIFE SABON THE LITTLE PRINCE

This project involved the design of a magazine inspired by the famous book The Little Prince, conceived as a graphic journey through its world. Through editorial design, we aimed to visually reinterpret the story, its characters, and the values it represents: imagination, friendship, innocence, and the importance of seeing beyond what is visible.

The magazine was designed as a colorful, dynamic, and playful experience, reflecting the poetic and symbolic nature of the book. Each page was carefully crafted to guide the reader through the different worlds and emotions present in the story, combining illustration, color, and editorial composition.

It was a group project, which allowed us to learn how to collaborate, share ideas, and unify different design approaches into a coherent final result. Additionally, this was our first time working with Adobe InDesign, marking the beginning of our exploration into the world of editorial design and opening the door to a new creative discipline.

This project became more than a technical exercise; it was a creative and narrative experience where design served as a storytelling tool, paying tribute to the timeless universe of The Little Prince.
