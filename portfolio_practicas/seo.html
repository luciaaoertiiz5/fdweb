LUZ ZIPP REVOLUTION 4 SEASONS UNSEEN SEO BIRDLIFE SABON THE LITTLE PRINCE

This project focuses on the rebranding of SEO/BirdLife, a non-governmental organization dedicated to the protection of nature and biodiversity. The goal of this redesign was to bring light, clarity, and modernity to the brand, creating a more complete and contemporary visual identity while staying true to its core values.


The rebranding introduces a cleaner, more flexible, and modern visual system, designed to adapt to digital and print media. Color, typography, and graphic elements were carefully chosen to reflect nature, transparency, and accessibility.
