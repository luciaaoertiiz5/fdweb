My works

The Little Prince

This project is a travel magazine inspired by the original story of The Little Prince by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry. The publication reinterprets the Little Prince’s journeys as tourist destinations, inviting the reader to explore planets and emotions as if they were real places to discover. Each page pays tribute to the poetic and philosophical spirit of the work, combining editorial design, illustration, and storytelling to create a unique visual and emotional experience. A travel guide to the invisible, the essential, and the eternal.

How would it be to accompany the Little Prince on his adventures from a different perspective?

Zipp Revolution

Zipp Revolution is not just a cycling shop; it is a digital experience designed for true two-wheel enthusiasts. This project features an e-commerce website where design, speed, and the thrill of cycling come together. Through a clean and dynamic interface, users can explore high-end products as if they were riding a professional race route. More than selling bikes, Zipp Revolution conveys the feeling of freedom and power that only cycling can offer.

Can a website make you feel like you're riding a high-end bike?
