ZIPP REVOLUTION 4 SEASONS UNSEEN SEO BIRDLIFE SABON THE LITTLE PRINCE

Unseen is a deeply personal photography project inspired by my experiences with depression and anxiety. Through this work, I wanted to explore and visually express the emotional journey of facing what is often invisible to others.


The series is mostly in black and white, reflecting the intensity and depth of these emotions. Cold, foggy landscapes create a sense of isolation, while the central figure moves through different emotional stages.
