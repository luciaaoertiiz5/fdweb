LUZ

LUZ is a premium editorial brand identity that explores the intersection of movement and luminance. This project features the inaugural Issue Nº1 of a photography-focused magazine, alongside a complete stationery system.


The visual language is built upon a rhythmic stripe system and a minimalist typographic approach, capturing the energy of long-exposure photography.
