LUZ is a premium editorial brand identity that explores the intersection of movement and luminance. This project features the inaugural Issue Nº1 of a photography-focused magazine, alongside a complete stationery system including special edition notebooks, planners, and bookmarks.


The visual language is built upon a rhythmic stripe system and a minimalist typographic approach, capturing the energy of long-exposure photography. From its vivid color palettes to its "Vogue-inspired" editorial layout, LUZ represents a commitment to clarity, technical sophistication, and high-end design.
