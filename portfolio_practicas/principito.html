This project involved the design of a magazine inspired by the famous book The Little Prince, conceived as a graphic journey through its world.

The magazine was designed as a colorful, dynamic, and playful experience, reflecting the poetic and symbolic nature of the book. Each page was carefully crafted to guide the reader through different worlds.

It was a group project, marking the beginning of our exploration into editorial design with Adobe InDesign.

This project became more than a technical exercise; it was a creative and narrative experience where design served as a storytelling tool.
